Alternating Jump Lunges

Start in a split lunge position with left leg forward and both knees bent at 90 degrees, arms at sides. Swing arms upward while explosively driving off the ground, switching feet in mid-air. Land softly with right leg forward and left leg back, both knees bent into a lunge. Continue jumping off the ground, switching front foot each time.

Jumping Rope

Stand with feet hip-width apart, holding one jump rope handle in each hand, arms at sides and elbows tucked in close to ribs. Swing jump rope up and over head to begin. Jump rope as fast as possible, bouncing lightly on the balls of feet in between jumps. Keep shoulder blades down and back, and rotate the rope with wrists, not arms.
