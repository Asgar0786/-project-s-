Short bursts of intense exercise

About

Hello! welcome HIIT stands for high-intensity interval training. Short bursts of intense exercise. It's a type of cardio exercise that involves short, intense bursts of exercise. HIIT workouts can be performed at home or at the gym, and require little to no equipment.

Types

Sprinting

Sprinting is an exercise that involves running at your top speed for a short distance. It's an anaerobic exercise that uses quick bursts of energy without oxygen.

High knees

Stand with your feet hip-width apart. Lift up your left knee to your chest. Switch to lift your right knee to your chest. Continue the movement, alternating legs and moving at a sprinting or running pace..

Weightlifting circuits

Weightlifting circuits, also known as circuit training, are a series of exercises that target different muscle groups with little rest in between.

Benifits

Sprinting

Efficient at burning fat. Increased metabolic rate. Improved cardiovascular health. Enhanced muscular strength and tone. Boosted endurance and performance. Time efficiency. Harder, better, faster, stronger.

High knees

High knees activate your quadriceps, hamstrings, calves, glutes, and hip flexors, helping improve muscular endurance, balance, and coordination in these muscles.

Weightlifting circuits

Circuit training involves resistance exercises that work multiple muscle groups, it helps to build overall strength and improves muscular endurance./p>
