About

Hello! welcome Bicep Workout By doing bicep exercises, our arms also remain strong and our biceps also gain and become stronger. Here are some biceps exercises: which you can do at home and are also easy.

Types

Close-Grip Barbell Curl

Grasp a barbell with your hands inside shoulder-width. Start with your arms extended and the bar in front of your thighs. Curl the bar up as high as possible without letting your elbows come forward. Slowly lower the bar back down to full extension.

Conventional Barbell Curl

Grasp a barbell with an underhand (supinated), shoulder-width grip. Start with your arms extended and the bar in front of your thighs. Curl the bar up as high as possible without letting your elbows come forward. Slowly lower the bar back down to full extension.

Hammer Curl

Stand holding a dumbbell in each hand with your palms facing your sides (neutral position). Curl the weights up while keeping your forearms in the neutral (aka hammer) position throughout. Hold the top for a moment and squeeze your biceps and forearm muscles. Lower the weights back down under control to full elbow extension.
